Our Delicous Menu

Discover the flavors of Indonesia with our curated selection of today’s finest offerings. From rich, aromatic coffee brews to delicious local delicacies, our menu is crafted to bring you the best of Indonesian cuisine in every bite and sip. Enjoy fresh, authentic tastes that celebrate our heritage, all made with the highest quality ingredients.

Kopi Luwak

Rp 14.999

Kopi Luwak Bali is a premium coffee from Bali, Indonesia. It’s made from beans eaten and excreted by civet cats, resulting in a smooth, rich flavor with unique characteristics.

Kopi Tarik

Rp 14.999

Kopi Tarik is a traditional coffee drink from Aceh, Indonesia. It’s made by pouring coffee back and forth between two containers to create a frothy, smooth texture. The drink combines strong brewed coffee with sweetened condensed milk.

Kopi Tubruk

Rp 9.999

Kopi Tubruk is a traditional Indonesian coffee from Java. It’s made by boiling coarsely ground coffee beans with water, resulting in a strong, rich flavor with grounds often settling at the bottom of the cup.

Kopi Walik

Rp 9.999

Kopi Walik is a unique Indonesian coffee from Flores, processed by fermenting beans eaten and excreted by civet cats. This method imparts a distinct, smooth flavor to the coffee.

Kueh Jedah

Rp 9.999

Kueh Jedah is a traditional Acehnese sweet made from glutinous rice, coconut milk, and palm sugar. It has a sticky texture and rich, sweet flavor, often enjoyed during special occasions.

Kacang Rebus

Rp 9.999

Kacang Rebus is a popular Indonesian snack made from boiled peanuts. It’s a simple, healthy treat with a soft texture and naturally savory flavor, often enjoyed as a light snack or side dish.

Kueh Pancong

Rp 9.999

Kueh Pancong is a traditional Indonesian cake made from rice flour, coconut milk, and grated coconut. Cooked in a special mold, it's crispy outside, soft inside, and often served with sugar on top.

Singkong Goreng

Rp 9.999

Singkong Goreng dengan Sambal is a savory Indonesian snack featuring deep fried cassava served with spicy sambal sauce. The crispy cassava slices complement the flavorful heat of the sambal, creating a delicious and satisfying combination enjoyed as a snack or side dish.
